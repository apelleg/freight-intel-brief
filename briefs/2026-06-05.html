📋 Regulatory Watch

Gen Re (reinsurance) published June 2, 2026 analysis confirming the Montgomery SCOTUS ruling leaves freight brokers with zero mandatory federal liability insurance — only a $75,000 surety bond that does not respond to personal injury or wrongful death judgments. The insurance market has not yet repriced this exposure; when it does, small and mid-tier brokers without systematic, documented carrier-vetting processes face structural existential cost changes. This dynamic accelerates consolidation toward large, well-capitalized brokers — including Uber Freight (June 2, 2026 — Gen Re).

The SELF DRIVE Act (H.R. 7390) is advancing toward full House Energy & Commerce Committee markup — would preempt all state AV restrictions and allow AV manufacturers to self-certify without NHTSA pre-market review, potentially creating a unified national framework that accelerates Aurora and Waabi commercial deployment to a 50-state market by 2027 (June 2026 — Congress.gov).
