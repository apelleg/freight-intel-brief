⚡ Today's Top Signal

A US-Iran peace deal to reopen the Strait of Hormuz is "largely negotiated," per President Trump on May 23, with announcement expected imminently. Under the framework, Iran would clear Hormuz mines and reopen the strait toll-free for 60 days while Washington lifts its port blockade and eases oil sanctions.
