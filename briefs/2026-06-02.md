### ⚡ Today's Top Signal

### 💡 CPO Lens: 3 Things Worth Discussing with Your Team

1. **ShipStation Global's Omnimodal Threat:** A Thoma Bravo-backed platform now offers TL + LTL + parcel to 3M customers through a single software interface — what does Uber Freight's product need to do differently to win on something other than carrier capacity and price? What's our durable moat for the enterprise shipper who can now consolidate their entire freight spend with a software-first competitor?

2. **Aurora Gen-2 Driverless Launch in Q2:** When Aurora's Gen-2 hardware ships and observer-free trucks are ready at scale, Uber Freight (a named Aurora customer) should have a product strategy ready — how do we price, load-match, and operationalize driverless truck capacity so we capture the margin, not just route the load?

3. **Amazon Prime Day June 23 — Load Surge in 3 Weeks:** With the FBA inbound cutoff already passed, the demand conversion from ocean to domestic inland is arriving now. Does Uber Freight's dynamic pricing and real-time carrier matching system have what it takes to capture the surge premium during the year's first major demand spike under new leadership?
